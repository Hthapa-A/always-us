Our Journey

2019 — When We Met

Life was heavy back then. I had just appeared in my Grade 11 board exams and scored well. My old school even awarded me a 25,000 NPR cash prize, which I went back to claim during Dashain in Chautara. I was just trying to survive — no big dreams, no romantic thoughts.

While standing in the queue at Nepal Bank, cheque in my hand, I saw you. A familiar face I had once seen during the SEE exams. You were there with your mom. We didn’t speak. Yet something shifted inside me.

That ordinary moment became the beginning of an extraordinary story. A light appeared in my dark, uncertain world.

2024 — Distance Began

We talked every night from 9 to 10 PM. The rest of the day, we studied with dedication. We were young, focused, and unknowingly falling in love.

Then the world stopped. COVID-19 shattered plans, delayed dreams, and tested patience. You aimed for BDS. I dreamed of becoming an engineer. Suddenly, everything felt uncertain.

We cried together. We laughed through tears. Even without words, we knew love was there — silently holding us close.

2025 — Still Choosing Each Other

Failures came one after another. Army attempts. Exams. Rejections. I broke down completely. I felt like I had failed my family, myself, and my future.

But you never left. Your words healed what no medicine ever could. You believed in me when I had nothing left to believe in.

In May, something magical happened. You confessed that you loved me. After waiting so long, I finally had you. ALsA♥️, you became my strength, my calm, my reason to keep going.

2026 — Almost Home

I came to the UK. Life taught me the real value of time, money, family, and loved ones. Every day, I am quietly growing. Silently fighting battles.

Distance is still here — but it’s losing. The end is visible. Every sacrifice is finally making sense.

2027 & Beyond — Always Together

No more countdowns. No more waiting. Just ordinary mornings, shared dreams, and a peaceful life.

Distance didn’t break us. It trained us.
